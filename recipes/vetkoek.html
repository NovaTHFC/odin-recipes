Vetkoek/Amagwinya

Description

This dish is a traditional South African crispy fried bread which extends across the country. You can make them any size and enjoy then with any filling you like

This dish is fast, simple and tasty!

Indgredients

  • 2 cups lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 packet of instant yeast (10g)
  • 7 cups of all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 cups of oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix lukewarm water, sugar and yeast in a small bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and bubbles slighty (5 minutes).
    2. Sift flout and salt together in a large bowl.
  2. Pour the water over the flour abd knead til dough is smooth and elastic. Cover bowl with a clean cloth and let the dough rise for about 45 minutes.
  3. Seperate dough into small balls and roll until smooth. Flatten ball until its the size of your palm.
  4. Heat oil in a large pot or pan until 175 celcuis
  5. Fry in hot oil, 2-3 pieces at a time, until golden brown. Pat with paper towels once done to remove excess oil.
  6. Serve with butter or any filling of choice and enjoy.
