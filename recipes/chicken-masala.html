Chicken masala

Description

This dish is a classic indian with flavoursome chicken coated with a spicy and thick masala sauce. This comforting dish comes all together in 45 minutes and makes for a satisfying meal.

Chicken masala goes best with rice, rooti or naan.

Indgredients

  • 1/2kg chicken
  • 3/4 teaspoon ginger and garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon tumeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon garam masala or chicken masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder(or less spicy chilli powder)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 piece cinnamon
  • 1 cup of onions
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
