Chicken masala

Description

This dish is a classic indian with flavoursome chicken coated with a spicy and thick masala sauce. This comforting dish comes all together in 45 minutes and makes for a satisfying meal.

Chicken masala goes best with rice, rooti or naan.

Indgredients

  • 1/2kg chicken
  • 2x 3/4 teaspoon ginger and garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon tumeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon garam masala or chicken masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder(or less spicy chilli powder)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 piece cinnamon
  • 1 cup of onions
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • coriander leaves
  • mint leaves

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl add the chicken, ginger and galric paste, tumeric, salt, garam masala and the chilli powder.
  2. Mix well and marinate for 1 hour or over night.
  3. Heat a poit with oil and add the bay leaf, cardamons,cloves and cinnamon.
  4. Add the cup of onions.
  5. Saute onions for 5-6 minutes, after that, reduce heat and continue to saute until they're golden.
  6. Add ginger and garlic paste and saute until aromatic smell is gone.
  7. Add tomato puree then add 1/2 teaspoon of chili powder, 3/4 teaspoon of masala and 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  8. Saute until mixture turns thick.
  9. Add the marinated chicken and fry on a slightly higher heat for 5 minutes.
  10. Cover and cook for 5 minutes on low heat so the flavour is absorbed.
  11. Chop handfull of coriander and mint leaves and add to the pan and stir.
  12. Cover until chicken is soft and tender to your liking.
  13. Garnish with ocriander and serve with side of your liking.
